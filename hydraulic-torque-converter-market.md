<p><strong>The Hydraulic Torque Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque converter market consists of two main types: single-stage and multistage hydraulic torque converters. Single-stage converters are designed for applications requiring less torque multiplication and are typically simpler, lighter, and more cost-effective. In contrast, multistage hydraulic torque converters incorporate multiple turbine and impeller stages, enabling higher torque multiplication and efficiency, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications such as in industrial machinery and high-performance vehicles. Both types enhance vehicle performance and improve fuel efficiency in various automotive applications.</p></p>
