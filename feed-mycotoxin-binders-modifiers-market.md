<p><p>The Feed Mycotoxin Binders and Modifiers market primarily consists of two types: feed mycotoxin binders and feed mycotoxin modifiers. Mycotoxin binders are substances that adsorb toxins produced by molds in feed, preventing their absorption in animal gastrointestinal tracts. They help reduce the harmful effects of mycotoxins on animal health and productivity. On the other hand, mycotoxin modifiers are additives that detoxify or alter the chemical structure of mycotoxins, mitigating their toxic effects and improving feed safety and quality.</p></p>
