<p><strong>The PE Wax Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidation Type PE Wax</li><li>Non-oxidation Type PE Wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE wax market consists of two primary types: oxidation type PE wax and non-oxidation type PE wax. Oxidation type PE wax is produced through the oxidative degradation of polyethylene, resulting in enhanced properties such as adhesion and compatibility with various materials. In contrast, non-oxidation type PE wax is obtained without oxidation, maintaining the original polyethylene structure, making it suitable for applications requiring robust thermal stability and low viscosity. Both types serve distinct industrial purposes, catering to diverse needs in plastics, coatings, and lubricants.</p></p>
