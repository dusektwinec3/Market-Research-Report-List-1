<p><p>The food-grade sweet potato starch market is categorized into two primary types: fresh and dried. Dried sweet potato starch undergoes dehydration, extending its shelf life and enhancing its versatility in food applications like sauces, snacks, and baked goods. Both types are valued for their nutritional benefits, gluten-free properties, and natural thickening abilities in culinary practices.</p></p>
