<p><strong>The DNA Sequencing Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Maxam-Gilbert Sequencing</li><li>Chain-Termination Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA sequencing technologies encompass various methods for determining the order of nucleotides in DNA. The Maxam-Gilbert sequencing technique utilizes chemical reactions to cleave DNA at specific bases, generating fragments that can be analyzed. In contrast, chain-termination methods, like Sanger sequencing, involve the incorporation of modified nucleotides that terminate DNA synthesis, allowing for sequencing by size separation of resulting fragments. Both methods have significantly contributed to genomics, with distinct applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and biotechnology.</p></p>
