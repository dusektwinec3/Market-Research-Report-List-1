Nylon tire fabric is known for its strength and durability, offering excellent puncture resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for high-performance tires. Polyester tire fabric is lightweight and cost-effective, providing good dimensional stability and resistance to heat. Chafer tire fabric, often used in areas prone to abrasion, enhances tire durability by protecting sidewalls and bead areas. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing tire performance and longevity.</p></p>
