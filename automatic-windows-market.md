<p><p>Automatic windows can be categorized into three market types: electric, mechanical, and hybrid. Electric windows use motors for seamless opening and closing, offering convenience and automation. Mechanical windows rely on manual or spring-operated systems, providing simplicity and reliability without electrical components. Hybrid windows combine both systems, leveraging the benefits of electricity for ease while retaining some mechanical features for backup or manual operation. This diversification allows users to choose based on preferences for automation, functionality, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
