<p><p>The Plastic Clad Silica (PCS) Fiber market is categorized into two main types: UV/VIS and VIS/NIR. UV/VIS fibers are designed for applications in ultraviolet and visible light ranges, typically used in sensors and medical devices. On the other hand, VIS/NIR fibers cater to visible and near-infrared applications, making them suitable for telecommunications and industrial uses. Both types leverage the unique properties of silica core fibers, offering flexibility, durability, and cost-effectiveness in various optical applications.</p></p>
