<p><strong>The Bicycle Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT Fluid</li><li>Mineral Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle brake fluid market is primarily composed of two types: DOT fluid and mineral oil. DOT fluid, derived from glycol-based formulations, is commonly used in hydraulic braking systems, offering high boiling points and effective performance under varying temperatures. In contrast, mineral oil is derived from refined petroleum and is favored for its non-corrosive properties and compatibility with certain brake systems. Each type serves specific bike models and rider preferences, influencing performance and maintenance considerations within the cycling community.</p></p>
