<p><p>The Nitrogen Evaporators market comprises two primary types: dry evaporators and water bath heaters. Dry evaporators utilize ambient air to evaporate solvents from samples efficiently, making them suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Conversely, water bath heaters employ a heated water bath to provide a controlled temperature environment, ensuring uniform heating and effective solvent removal. Both types serve essential roles in laboratories, particularly in applications like sample concentration, protein precipitation, and solvent evaporation, catering to various research and analytical needs.</p></p>
