<p><ul><li>Shot Coke Type</li><li>Sponge Coke Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel-grade petcoke market is primarily divided into two types: shot coke and sponge coke. Shot coke is characterized by its hard, flaky particles with high carbon content, making it suitable for use in blast furnaces. In contrast, sponge coke has a more porous structure and lower density, allowing for improved combustion efficiency in power generation and cement production. The choice between these types depends on the specific industrial applications and the desired properties for optimal performance.</p></p>
