<p><strong>The PVDF Membrane Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PVDF Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PVDF Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDF membrane filters market encompasses two primary types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic PVDF membranes. Hydrophobic PVDF membranes are designed to repel water, making them ideal for gas filtration and the sterilization of air or other gases. In contrast, hydrophilic PVDF membranes are water-attracting, facilitating the filtration of aqueous solutions and biological samples. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, including pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and environmental sectors, owing to their chemical compatibility and excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
