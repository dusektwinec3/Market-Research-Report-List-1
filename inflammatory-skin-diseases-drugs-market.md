<p><p>The inflammatory skin diseases drugs market encompasses various therapeutic categories. Retinoids, derived from vitamin A, target skin cell growth and inflammation, often used for acne and psoriasis. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory agents that reduce redness and swelling, commonly prescribed for conditions like eczema. Calcineurin inhibitors work by modulating the immune response in the skin, effective for atopic dermatitis. Other treatments include antihistamines and biologics, addressing various inflammatory skin disorders through diverse mechanisms. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific conditions.</p></p>
