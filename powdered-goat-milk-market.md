<p><p>The powdered goat milk market is categorized into two main types: whole milk and skim milk. Whole powdered goat milk retains the fat content found in fresh goat milk, offering a rich flavor and creaminess, making it ideal for baking and cooking. Skim powdered goat milk, on the other hand, has had most of its fat removed, resulting in a lower-calorie option that still provides essential nutrients. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and dietary needs in the market.</p></p>
