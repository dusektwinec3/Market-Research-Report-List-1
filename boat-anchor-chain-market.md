<p><strong>The Boat Anchor Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Studless Anchor Chain</li><li>Studlink Anchor Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat anchor chain market is primarily categorized into studless and studlink anchor chains. Studless anchor chains are crafted from uniform links, providing higher flexibility and easier handling, making them suitable for smaller vessels. In contrast, studlink anchor chains have protruding studs that help prevent links from deforming under load, offering enhanced strength and durability, ideal for larger ships. Both types serve essential roles in anchoring marine vessels, but their applications vary based on size, weight, and operational requirements.</p></p>
