<p><p>Industrial manipulators are categorized into three main types: pneumatic, electronic, and hydraulic. Pneumatic manipulators utilize compressed air for movement and are known for their lightweight and quick operation, ideal for handling lighter loads. Electronic manipulators leverage electric motors and advanced control systems, allowing for precise movements and automation. Hydraulic manipulators employ fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering high power and stability, making them suitable for heavy industrial tasks. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational needs.</p></p>
