<p><p>The needle retractable safety syringe market comprises two primary types: manual retractable safety syringes and auto-retractable safety syringes. Manual retractable safety syringes require the user to manually retract the needle after use, enhancing safety by preventing needle-stick injuries. In contrast, auto-retractable safety syringes feature a built-in mechanism that automatically retracts the needle after injection, providing a more convenient and efficient solution. Both types are designed to improve safety for healthcare professionals and patients by minimizing risks associated with needle disposal.</p></p>
