<p><strong>The Urinary Protein Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA Based Reagent</li><li>Colorimetric Method Based Reagent</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urinary protein reagent market includes various types designed for protein detection in urine. ELISA-based reagents utilize enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays for high specificity and sensitivity, making them ideal for clinical diagnostics. Colorimetric method-based reagents employ color changes to indicate protein presence, offering a straightforward and rapid assessment. Other methods encompass immunochromatographic tests and biosensors, providing alternative approaches for protein quantification. Each type caters to different diagnostic needs and preferences, enhancing the overall testing landscape.</p></p>
