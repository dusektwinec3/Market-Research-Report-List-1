<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Sterilization Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Sterilization Equipment</li><li>Chemical Sterilization</li><li>Filtration Sterilization</li><li>Ionizing Radiation Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical sterilization equipment market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Heat Sterilization Equipment** uses high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms, commonly employing methods like autoclaving.</p><p>2. **Chemical Sterilization** involves using chemical agents, such as ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide, to achieve sterility at lower temperatures.</p><p>3. **Filtration Sterilization** employs physical barriers, filtering out contaminants from liquids and gases, commonly used in heat-sensitive products.</p><p>4. **Ionizing Radiation Sterilization** uses gamma rays or electron beams to disrupt the DNA of microorganisms, providing effective sterilization without heat.</p></p>
