<p><p>The Vacuum Photocell market offers two primary types: center cathode photocells and center anode photocells. Center cathode photocells operate by using a cathode placed at the center of the device, effectively enhancing sensitivity to light and enabling efficient photoelectric conversion. In contrast, center anode photocells feature an anode at the center, providing a different configuration that influences responsiveness and performance in various applications. Both types are utilized across industries for their effectiveness in light sensing and control mechanisms.</p></p>
