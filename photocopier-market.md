<p><p>The photocopier market can be categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Photochemical**: Utilizes light-sensitive chemicals for image reproduction, often seen in older technologies.</p><p>2. **Thermal**: Employs heat to transfer images onto paper, commonly used in laser printers.</p><p>3. **Xerographic**: Based on electrostatic charges, this widely used method creates copies by transferring toner onto paper.</p><p>4. **Digital**: Involves direct image capture and digital processing, allowing for faster, high-quality copies and integration with electronic devices.</p></p>
