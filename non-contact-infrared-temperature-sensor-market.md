<p><p>The non-contact infrared temperature sensor market includes two main types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole Thermopile Sensors are designed for mounting in drilled holes, offering robust performance in various applications. In contrast, SMD Thermopile Sensors are surface-mounted, providing a compact design ideal for modern electronics. Both types leverage thermopile technology to measure temperature without direct contact, making them suitable for diverse applications such as health monitoring, industrial processes, and automation systems.</p></p>
