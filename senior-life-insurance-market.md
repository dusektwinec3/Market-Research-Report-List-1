<p><ul><li>Permanent Life Insurance</li><li>Term Life Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The senior life insurance market encompasses two main types: permanent and term life insurance. Permanent life insurance provides lifelong coverage, accumulating cash value over time, making it suitable for seniors seeking long-term financial security and investment benefits. In contrast, term life insurance offers coverage for a specific period, typically at lower premiums, ideal for seniors who need protection for a set timeframe, such as covering final expenses or ensuring income for dependents. Both options cater to diverse financial needs.</p></p>
