<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Imaging Spectroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visible/near-Infrared(VNIR)</li><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The imaging spectroscopy market encompasses various segments based on spectral ranges. Visible/Near-Infrared (VNIR) focuses on capturing light reflecting from surfaces, useful for agriculture and mineral exploration. Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR) detects moisture and chemical compositions, aiding in environmental monitoring. Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) plays a crucial role in thermal imaging, important for defense and security applications. Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR) specializes in thermal emissions, beneficial for night vision and industrial inspections. Each type serves unique applications across multiple industries.</p></p>
