<p><p>The cautery pencil market is segmented into two primary types: hand control and foot control. Hand control cautery pencils are operated manually, allowing surgeons to activate the device using their grip and finger pressure, providing precise control during procedures. In contrast, foot control cautery pencils enable users to operate the device with their foot, freeing their hands for other tasks. This distinction offers flexibility and choice in operating room setups, catering to the preferences and needs of surgeons during surgical procedures.</p></p>
