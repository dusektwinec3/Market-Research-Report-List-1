<p><ul><li>C5 Resins</li><li>C9 Resins</li><li>Hydrogenated Hydrocarbon Resins</li><li>C5/C9 Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum-based hydrocarbon resin market is categorized into several types, including C5 resins, C9 resins, hydrogenated hydrocarbon resins, and C5/C9 resins. C5 resins are derived from cyclopentadiene and are known for their quick drying and adhesive properties. C9 resins, sourced from aromatic hydrocarbons, offer excellent hardness and thermal stability. Hydrogenated hydrocarbon resins provide superior clarity and UV resistance, making them ideal for various applications. Lastly, C5/C9 resins combine the benefits of both C5 and C9 types, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
